FAQ's

How Long Is The Recovery?
You will be able to see without glasses but it will still be hazy or misty immediately after the procedure. Vision will start to get better the following day. It will usually take 1 – 2 days for functional vision to return.
Will There Be Any Side Effects?
The most common side effects are dry eyes, light sensitivity, glare and halos during the first few weeks, but most of these side effects will subside over time. For dryness, it can easily be resolved by using lubricating eye drops. As for the starbursts and halos, it will resolve with time and can take between nine months to a year.
Is The Procedure Painful?
There is no pain at all during the procedure. There is a mild discomfort for the next few hours as the anesthesia fades away.
